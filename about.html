About Crimson Crown

The driving force behind South Asia's competitive gaming future.

Our Story

Crimson Crown was founded by a small group of passionate gamers who saw a gap in the South Asian esports scene. While talent was abundant, consistent and professional platforms for competition were scarce. We took it upon ourselves to build what was missing.

Starting with weekly Farlight 84 scrims, we've grown into a trusted tournament organizer, dedicated to creating a structured and rewarding environment for players to hone their skills and compete at the highest level.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to elevate the esports ecosystem in South Asia by providing reliable, professional, and accessible competitive platforms. We aim to nurture talent, foster sportsmanship, and build a thriving community.

Our vision is to see South Asian teams and players achieve global recognition. We are building the foundational infrastructure to help our region's talent shine on the world stage.
