About Crimson Crown

The driving force behind South Asia's competitive gaming future.

Our Story: From Passion to Platform

Crimson Crown was born from a simple observation: South Asia was filled with immense gaming talent, but lacked the professional infrastructure for that talent to flourish. A small group of passionate gamers and community builders, we saw an opportunity to create something meaningful.

We began by organizing weekly Farlight 84 scrims, focusing on fair play, consistent scheduling, and clear communication. What started as a small initiative quickly grew into a trusted community. Today, we stand as an established tournament organizer, committed to creating a structured and rewarding environment where players can hone their skills, compete at the highest level, and begin their journey to becoming professional athletes.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to elevate the esports ecosystem in South Asia by providing reliable, professional, and accessible competitive platforms. We aim to nurture talent, foster sportsmanship, and build a thriving community where every player has the opportunity to succeed.

Our vision is to see South Asian teams and players achieve global recognition. We are building the foundational infrastructure—from grassroots scrims to premier tournaments—to help our region's talent shine on the world stage.
